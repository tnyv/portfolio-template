About me

Hi, my name is Tony. I began coding in 2014. My passion for programming was sparked after I built my first game in C++. I fell in love with the concept of creating something from nothing. Over the years, I've programmed with more languages like Java, JavaScript, C#, PHP, and SQL --making projects for school and myself. Nowadays, my focus and specialty is the web stack.

My main objective is to acquire an entry-level front-end or back-end web developer position. I'm currently employed in IT as a systems analyst, and even though I enjoy my current job, I believe that the next step in my career is to become a software developer. I'm eager to join a dev team to learn all that there is about software development and deployment.
